Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Passion

TG Cabs was founded in 2012 by Rajinikanth Mukkidi with a single Innova and a big dream — to give every Indian traveller a safe, comfortable, and affordable journey. What started as a one-car service has grown into a trusted brand with 20+ vehicles covering 50+ destinations across India.

We've carried newborns home from hospitals, driven families on dream vacations, and helped thousands reach holy temples with devotion. Every trip is personal to us — your journey matters.
